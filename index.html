At the end of a month and a day of my punishment, I was released. For a month and a day I did not hold my daughter, who was born two weeks before I went into isolation. Outside the closed room waited - not always patiently - four children and a wife after birth. I was like a prisoner waiting for his verdict on the cell phone display. Every time the doctor gave me permission, I went out - wearing gloves and a mask - to my Zinzana and went to the 'get tested and drive' station to discuss an arrest extension. At the station it is forbidden to open windows or turn on an air conditioner, and you are only allowed to get cooked slowly inside the hot box, waiting for people in suits to push sticks through the window for you. After the test, you go back to your cell and wait for the results. I have been examined five times, Honorable Judge. Positive - the five stages of mourning Negative - Press conference Positive - Severe damage to public trust Negative - learn from the past Negative - Thank you very much Your Honor, I'm going to hold my daughter.
